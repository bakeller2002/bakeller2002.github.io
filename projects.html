OpenSim Deep Learning Pipeline

FAMM Lab · Jun 2025 - Present

Built deep learning models that predict lower-limb muscle and joint contact forces from ground reaction forces alone. First implemented to give a robotic controller live feedback, later generalized into a broader biomechanics tool. Designed scalable preprocessing for multi-modal data (motion capture + force plates) and generated ground-truth force labels by simulating every trial in OpenSim with a custom static-optimization routine, validated across younger and older adult populations.

code ↗ poster ↗ paper — in progress

Automated Nursery Viability System

Deep Learning Certificate Capstone · Jan 2026 - Present

Built a computer vision framework that automates plant viability grading at commercial nurseries, replacing subjective manual assessment with objective, scalable forecasting across large product volumes. Interviewed nursery staff on-site to translate their qualitative grading criteria into precise technical specifications the system could meet in a real production environment.

Esophageal SpO₂ Monitor

BME Senior Capstone · Jan - Nov 2024

Led software development for a multidisciplinary team building a novel esophageal SpO₂ monitor - developing embedded firmware in CircuitPython and a user-facing UI for the device. Supported component selection and sensor integration, and contributed to the design documentation and verification/validation protocols required to meet FDA and ISO 13485 standards.
